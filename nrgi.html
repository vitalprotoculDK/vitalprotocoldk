In modern life, the most common response to fatigue has become caffeine. Coffee, energy drinks, "boost" tablets... Most of us barely stay upright with 3-4 cups a day. But this isn't a cheap solution.

The real need isn't an energy "stimulant" — it's support for the body's own energy production system. Vitamin C, glycine and natural polyphenols are some of the components that play roles in this system.
